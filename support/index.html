Notes for measurement tools

Some Indie Apps Lab tools measure aspects of your environment, such as sound level. For these apps, please keep in mind:

  • Readings may differ between devices because microphones and sensors are different and often tuned by the manufacturer.
  • These apps are designed for indicative and educational use, not as certified professional measurement instruments.

If you rely on measurements for safety, regulation or professional work, please use certified equipment alongside our apps.
