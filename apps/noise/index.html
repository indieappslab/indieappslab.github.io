Sound Meter (Noise Level)

Sound Meter helps you quickly understand how loud your environment is. It shows real-time decibel (dB) readings, a noise chart and lets you keep a history of your measurements.

  • Live sound level in dB
  • Session history and details
  • Export to file
  • No account or sign-in required
