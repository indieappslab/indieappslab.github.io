Last updated: 2025-11-27

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sound Meter app by Indie Apps Lab handles your data.

1. Data we collect

The app does not collect, store or transmit any personal data to our servers. All sound measurements are processed directly on your device.

  • The microphone is used only to measure sound levels in real time.
  • Audio is not recorded or saved as sound files by the app.
  • Measurement history is stored locally on your device and is not automatically uploaded anywhere.

2. Permissions

The app requires microphone access to function as a sound level meter. If you deny this permission, the app will not be able to measure noise.

3. Third-party services

The current version of the app does not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Note: if a future version includes ads or analytics, this policy will be updated and clearly reflected here and on the app store listing.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade or share any personal data with third parties. Since the app does not send your measurement data to our servers, there is nothing for us to share.

5. Your choices

  • You can revoke microphone permission at any time in your Android system settings.
  • You can clear app data in system settings to remove all locally stored measurement history.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the app, you can contact us:

Email: indieappslab+support@gmail.com

By using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the app and stop using it.
