Privacy Policy – Pickly

Last updated: 2026-04-03

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Pickly mobile application (“Pickly”, “the App”) developed by Indie Apps Lab (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles your information.

Pickly is a simple, fast and intuitive app designed to help you make decisions instantly — whether by tapping, spinning, or shaking your device.

Privacy and simplicity are core principles of this App. We do not collect personal data, we do not track users, and we do not store your data on external servers.

2. Key Points at a Glance

  • No accounts. No sign-in, no registration.
  • No tracking. No analytics, no behavioral tracking.
  • No ads. The App does not include advertising SDKs.
  • No cloud storage. All app data stays on your device.
  • No third-party SDKs.
  • Offline-friendly. Works without internet connection.

3. Data Stored by the App

3.1 Decisions and Custom Options (Local Storage)

  • The App may store your custom choices, lists, or recent decisions.
  • This data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not upload or synchronize this data to any server.

3.2 App Settings

  • Preferences such as theme, language, sounds, animations, and interaction settings are stored locally.
  • These settings are never transmitted to us.

3.3 Personal Data

Pickly does not require your name, email address, phone number or any personal information.

We do not collect or store personal data.

4. Device Features and Permissions

The App does not request access to sensitive data.

  • No access to contacts
  • No access to location
  • No access to microphone
  • No access to photos or files

Some features (such as shake-to-decide) use motion sensors on your device. This data is processed locally and never leaves your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Pickly does not use analytics, ads, tracking tools, or third-party SDKs.

6. Data Sharing

  • No data is sold
  • No data is shared
  • No data is transferred

7. Data Retention

  • All data stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Your Rights

  • Full control over your data
  • Delete anytime by clearing app data

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The latest version will always be shown here.
