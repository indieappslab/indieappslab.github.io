Privacy Policy – Decision Maker

Last updated: 2026-04-03

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Decision Maker mobile application (“Decision Maker”, “the App”) developed by Indie Apps Lab (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles your information.

Decision Maker is a simple utility app designed to help users make random selections and decisions using customizable options and interactive input methods.

Privacy and simplicity are core principles of this App. We do not collect personal data, we do not track users, and we do not store your data on external servers.

2. Key Points at a Glance

  • No accounts. No sign-in, no registration.
  • No tracking. No analytics, no behavioral tracking.
  • No ads. The App does not include advertising SDKs.
  • No cloud storage. All app data stays on your device.
  • No third-party analytics or marketing SDKs.
  • Offline-friendly functionality.

3. Data Stored by the App

3.1 Decision Data and Custom Options (Local Storage)

  • The App may store your custom choices, lists, preferences, recent decisions, or other app-related content that you create inside the App.
  • This data is stored locally on your device in the App’s private storage.
  • We do not upload, sync or back up this data to our servers.

3.2 App Settings

  • Preferences such as theme, language, sounds, animations, app behavior, shake-related settings and other optional features may be stored locally.
  • These settings are never transmitted to us.

3.3 Personal Data

Decision Maker does not require your name, email address, phone number or other personally identifiable information to function.

We do not collect, process or store personal data on our servers.

4. Device Features and Permissions

The App is designed to function without collecting personal data and without requesting sensitive permissions unless clearly required by a feature.

  • No access to contacts.
  • No access to location.
  • No access to microphone.
  • No access to photos or media files.
  • No account or identity access.

Some interactive features, such as shake-based input, may use on-device motion sensors. Sensor input is processed locally on your device and is not sent to us or to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

Decision Maker does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking frameworks or marketing SDKs.

The App does not require an internet connection for its core functionality.

6. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell user data.
  • We do not share user data with third parties.
  • We do not transfer user data internationally.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All App data remains on your device until you delete it, clear the App’s storage or uninstall the App.
  • You have full control over your stored options, decisions and preferences.

8. Children’s Privacy

Decision Maker does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction).

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, including under applicable privacy laws such as GDPR, you may have rights related to your data.

  • Access – Your app data is stored locally on your device.
  • Deletion – You can remove all data by clearing storage or uninstalling the App.
  • Control – You can edit, reset or remove your local data at any time.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy if the App evolves.
  • The “Last updated” date will reflect the latest version.

11. Contact

Data controller: Indie Apps Lab

Email: indieappslab+support@gmail.com

By using Decision Maker, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
