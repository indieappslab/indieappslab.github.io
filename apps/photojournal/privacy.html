Privacy Policy – OneSnap

Last updated: 2026-04-06

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the OneSnap mobile application (“OneSnap”, “the App”) developed by Indie Apps Lab (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles your information.

OneSnap is a simple daily photo journal designed to help users capture one photo a day and build a personal visual memory over time.

Privacy and simplicity are core principles of this App. We do not collect personal data, we do not track users, and we do not store your photos or journal content on external servers.

2. Key Points at a Glance

  • No accounts. No sign-in, no registration.
  • No tracking. No analytics, no behavioral tracking.
  • No ads. The App does not include advertising SDKs.
  • No cloud storage. Your photos and entries stay on your device.
  • No third-party analytics or marketing SDKs.
  • Designed for private, on-device use.

3. Data Stored by the App

3.1 Daily Photos and Journal Entries (Local Storage)

  • The App stores your daily photos and any optional text entries, notes, captions, mood selections or related journal information.
  • This data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not upload, synchronize or back up this data to our servers.

3.2 App Settings

  • Preferences such as theme, reminders, notification settings, language and other in-app options are stored locally.
  • These settings are never transmitted to us.

3.3 Personal Data

OneSnap does not require your name, email address, phone number or any personally identifiable information to function.

We do not collect, process or store personal data on our servers.

4. Device Permissions

OneSnap is designed to work primarily on-device and only uses permissions that are relevant to its core photo journal functionality.

  • Camera – may be used to let you take your daily photo inside the App.
  • Photos / Media Library – may be used if you choose to save, access or manage your photo entries on your device.
  • Notifications – may be used for optional daily reminders.

These permissions are used only for the App’s intended features and operate on your device. We do not use them to collect or transmit your content to external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

OneSnap does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking frameworks or crash reporting services.

The App is designed to function without requiring cloud-based accounts or remote storage for its core experience.

6. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell user data.
  • We do not share user data with third parties.
  • We do not transfer user data internationally.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All data remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the App or clear the App’s storage.
  • You remain in control of your photos, entries and settings.

8. Children’s Privacy

OneSnap does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction).

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location (for example, under GDPR in the European Union), you may have rights related to data protection.

  • Access – Your data is stored locally on your device.
  • Deletion – Remove your data by deleting entries or uninstalling the App.
  • Control – Manage or reset your content and settings at any time.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy if the App evolves.
  • The “Last updated” date will reflect the latest version.

11. Contact

Data controller: Indie Apps Lab

Email: indieappslab+support@gmail.com

By using OneSnap, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
