Privacy Policy – OneSnap

Last updated: 2026-05-19

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the OneSnap mobile application (“OneSnap”, “the App”) developed by Indie Apps Lab (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles your information.

OneSnap is a private visual journal for keeping daily photos, notes, memories, and personal moments on your device.

Privacy and simplicity are core principles of this App. We do not collect personal data, we do not track users, and we do not store journal data on external servers.

2. Key Points at a Glance

  • No accounts. No sign-in, no registration.
  • No tracking. No analytics, no behavioral tracking.
  • No ads. The App does not include advertising SDKs.
  • No cloud storage. Journal content stays on your device.
  • No third-party analytics or marketing SDKs.
  • Full offline functionality.

3. Data Stored by the App

3.1 Journal Entries, Photos & Notes (Local Storage)

  • The App stores journal entries, selected photos, captions, dates, tags, and related personal notes.
  • This data is stored locally on your device in the App’s private storage.
  • We do not upload or synchronize this data to any server.

3.2 App Settings

  • Preferences such as theme, language, reminder options, and display settings are stored locally.
  • These settings are never transmitted to us.

3.3 Personal Data

OneSnap does not require your name, email address, phone number or any personally identifiable information.

We do not collect, process or store personal data on our servers.

4. Device Permissions

The App may ask for access to photos or camera features if you choose to add visual content to your journal.

  • Photo access is used only to let you choose images for journal entries.
  • Camera access, if enabled, is used only to capture a photo for an entry.
  • No access to microphone.
  • No access to location unless a future optional feature explicitly asks for it.
  • No access to contacts.

If optional reminders are enabled, they operate entirely on-device and are not connected to external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

OneSnap does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking frameworks or crash reporting services.

The App works fully offline and does not require internet connectivity for its core functionality.

6. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell user data.
  • We do not share user data with third parties.
  • We do not transfer user data internationally.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All data remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the App, or clear its storage.
  • You have full control over your journal content and settings.

8. Children’s Privacy

OneSnap does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction).

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., under GDPR in the European Union), you may have rights related to data protection.

  • Access – All data is stored locally on your device.
  • Deletion – Delete entries in the App or remove all data by uninstalling the App.
  • Control – Adjust or reset your data at any time.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy if the App evolves.
  • The “Last updated” date will reflect the latest version.

11. Contact

Data controller: Indie Apps Lab

Email: indieappslab+support@gmail.com

By using OneSnap, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
