Privacy Policy – Expiree

Last updated: 2026-03-31

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Expiree – Expiry Day Tracker mobile application (“Expiree”, “the App”) developed by Indie Apps Lab (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles your information.

Expiree is designed to help you track product and medication expiration dates, manage home inventory, and receive reminders before items expire.

Privacy and simplicity are core principles of this App. We do not collect personal data, we do not track users, and we do not store your data on external servers.

2. Key Points at a Glance

  • No accounts. No sign-in, no registration.
  • No tracking. No analytics, no behavioral tracking.
  • No ads. The App does not include advertising SDKs.
  • No cloud storage. All data stays on your device.
  • No third-party analytics or marketing SDKs.
  • Works offline. Internet connection is not required.

3. Data Stored by the App

3.1 Inventory Data (Local Storage)

  • The App stores information about your items such as product names, categories, expiration dates, quantities, locations, and optional notes.
  • This data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not upload or synchronize this data to any external servers.

3.2 Photos

  • If you choose to add photos to items, they are stored locally on your device.
  • Photos are never transmitted to us.

3.3 App Settings

  • Preferences such as theme, language, reminder settings, and storage locations are saved locally on your device.
  • These settings are not shared externally.

3.4 Personal Data

Expiree does not require your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.

We do not collect, process, or store personal data on our servers.

4. Device Permissions

The App may request certain permissions only when required for specific features.

  • Camera (optional): Used to take photos of items or scan barcodes.
  • Photos/Media (optional): Used to select images for items.
  • Notifications (optional): Used to remind you about upcoming expiration dates.

All permissions are optional and can be controlled via your device settings.

No permission data is transmitted to external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Expiree does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking frameworks.

The App does not require internet access for its core functionality.

6. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell user data.
  • We do not share user data with third parties.
  • We do not transfer user data internationally.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All data remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its storage.
  • You have full control over your data at all times.

8. Children’s Privacy

Expiree does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction).

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., under GDPR in the European Union), you may have rights related to data protection.

  • Access – All data is stored locally on your device.
  • Deletion – Remove all data by uninstalling the App.
  • Control – You can edit or delete your data at any time.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves.
  • The “Last updated” date will reflect the latest version.

11. Contact

Data controller: Indie Apps Lab

Email: indieappslab+support@gmail.com

By using Expiree, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
