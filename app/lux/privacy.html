Privacy Policy – Luma Lux

Last updated: 2026-01-10

This Privacy Policy explains how the Luma Lux app by Indie Apps Lab handles your data.

1. Data we collect

The app measures ambient light levels using your device’s built-in light sensor. Light readings are processed directly on your device.

  • Light sensor data is used only to show real-time illumination (lux) and charts inside the app.
  • No personal data is intentionally collected, stored, or transmitted to our own servers.
  • If the app includes measurement history, it is stored locally on your device and is not automatically uploaded.
  • If you use an export/share feature, the file is created on your device and shared only when you explicitly choose to share it.

2. Permissions

Luma Lux uses the device’s ambient light sensor. On most Android devices this sensor does not require a runtime permission prompt. If a future version adds optional features that require additional permissions, we will request them clearly and update this policy.

3. Third-party services

The current version of Luma Lux integrates with Firebase to help us improve app stability and fix bugs. Depending on the Firebase components enabled in the build (for example, Crashlytics / Analytics), Firebase may collect diagnostic and usage information such as device/app identifiers and technical details about crashes and app performance.

  • We use Firebase to receive crash reports and related diagnostics so we can identify and fix issues faster.
  • These reports are intended to contain technical information (e.g., app version, device model, OS version, crash stack trace).
  • Your light measurements (lux values, charts, history) are not intended to be included in Firebase reports.

Note: Firebase is a Google service and has its own privacy documentation and data handling practices.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. The app does not send your light measurement data to our servers. Limited diagnostic data may be processed by Firebase as described above to support app reliability.

5. Your choices

  • You can clear app data in your Android system settings to remove locally stored measurement history (if any).
  • You can uninstall the app at any time.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the app, you can contact us:

Email: indieappslab+support@gmail.com

By using Luma Lux, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the app and stop using it.
