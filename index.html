Who is behind the apps

About Indie Apps Lab

Indie Apps Lab is a one-person studio experimenting with small, carefully designed tools. The goal is simple: apps that feel calm, honest and useful.

Instead of trying to be everything at once, each app focuses on a single job: measure something, visualise it clearly, and stay out of your way. No feeds, no pressure, no gamified addiction loops.

This site is the home for all releases from the lab: app overviews, privacy policies and a direct contact channel when you need help.

What to expect

  • Small scope. Each app solves one concrete problem.
  • Respectful privacy. No accounts, no dark patterns.
  • Calm design. Clear information, minimal noise.
  • Indie pace. Features ship when they are ready, not rushed.
