About Indie Apps Lab

Indie Apps Lab is a one-person studio experimenting with small, carefully designed tools.

The goal is simple: apps that feel calm, honest and useful.

Each app focuses on one clear job — measure something, help you focus, or build better routines.

What to expect

  • Small scope. One clear problem.
  • Respectful privacy. No accounts.
  • Calm design. Minimal UI.
  • Indie pace. Features ship when ready.
